---
term: APOE
title: APOE (ε2 / ε3 / ε4)
category: interpreting
---
**APOE** is a gene whose ε-genotype is associated with Alzheimer's risk and lipid
metabolism. Your ε-type is determined by the combination of **two** SNPs:
`rs429358` and `rs7412`. Neither alone is enough.

If `rs429358` is missing from your file — which is common on consumer arrays —
your ε4 status is **indeterminate**, and genome-lens says exactly that instead of
guessing. APOE ε4 raises risk but is not deterministic; confirm with a
clinical-grade test and a clinician before drawing conclusions.
