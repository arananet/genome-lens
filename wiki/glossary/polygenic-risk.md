term: Polygenic risk
title: Polygenic risk
category: interpreting
---
Most common traits and conditions are **polygenic** — shaped by thousands of
variants each contributing a tiny amount, plus environment and chance. No single
SNP "causes" them.

genome-lens deliberately does **not** output a personal risk percentage. A
consumer array measures only a sliver of relevant variation, and polygenic risk
is a population-level tendency, not a personal prognosis or destiny.
