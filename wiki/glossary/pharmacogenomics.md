title: Pharmacogenomics
category: interpreting
---
**Pharmacogenomics** studies how genetic variation affects responses to
substances — caffeine metabolism, drug processing, and similar traits. Examples
in genome-lens include *CYP1A2* (`rs762551`) and *COMT* (`rs4680`).

These findings are educational. Real prescribing decisions use clinical-grade
pharmacogenomic panels interpreted by a clinician or pharmacist — never a
consumer array readout alone.
