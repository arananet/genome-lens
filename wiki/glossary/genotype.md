term: Genotype
title: Genotype
category: core
---
Your **genotype** at a SNP is the pair of alleles you carry there, written as two
letters such as `AA`, `AG`, or `GG`. Order is not meaningful, so `AG` and `GA`
describe the same genotype.

- **Homozygous** — two copies of the same allele (`AA` or `GG`).
- **Heterozygous** — two different alleles (`AG`).
- **No-call (`--`)** — the test could not read this position reliably.

genome-lens counts how many copies of the *effect allele* you carry to interpret
each finding.
