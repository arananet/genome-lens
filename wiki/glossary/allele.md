---
term: Allele
title: Allele
category: core
---
An **allele** is one of the alternative DNA letters that can occur at a given
position. At most SNPs there are two common alleles, for example **A** or **G**.

You inherit one allele from each parent, so at every position you carry two —
together they make your **genotype** (such as `AG`). The **effect allele** is the
specific allele a study associated with a trait or risk.
