---
term: No-call
title: No-call (--)
category: data-quality
---
A **no-call**, written `--`, means the test could not confidently determine your
genotype at that position. It is a gap in the data, not an indication of any
particular result.

genome-lens preserves no-calls rather than guessing, marks the finding
low-confidence, and tells you the position could not be read.
