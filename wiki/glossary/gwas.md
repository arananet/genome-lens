---
term: GWAS
title: Genome-Wide Association Study (GWAS)
category: interpreting
---
A **GWAS** scans many people's genomes to find SNPs statistically associated with
a trait or condition. Most associations it finds have **small** individual effects
and explain only a fraction of who develops the trait.

GWAS hits are the backbone of Tier B findings here. They are population-level
signals, not personal predictions — an associated allele shifts average odds
slightly, it does not decide your outcome.
