---
term: GRCh37
title: GRCh37 / build 37 (hg19)
category: data-quality
---
A **genome build** is the coordinate system that gives every position a
chromosome and a base-pair number. Consumer exports from 23andMe, AncestryDNA,
and MyHeritage are reported against **GRCh37** (also called build 37 or hg19).

genome-lens works entirely in GRCh37 so that file positions, knowledge-base
coordinates, and chromosome lengths all line up. Mixing builds would place
markers at the wrong locations.
